About

From the Apothecary

My healing journey began through pain, searching, and the feeling that there had to be something deeper than simply surviving. I was drawn to herbs, ritual, energy work, and intuitive healing because they helped me reconnect with myself in a way that felt real.

What began as curiosity became devotion. I started learning the language of plants, the emotional and spiritual atmosphere of healing, and how ritual, intention, and natural support could influence not just the body, but the soul and the energy of a space.

Awaken With Amber LLC was born from that journey. Amber's Alchemy Apothecary became the place where handcrafted remedies, spiritual guidance, botanical education, and custom healing experiences all came together.
